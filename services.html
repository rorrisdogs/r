Dog Training

How you live with your dog creates either a much-needed structured environment or lack thereof. A dog that understands "The House Rules" feels calm and secure. I teach you how to set up a structured environment and communicate these rules to your dog.

Dogs think differently than humans therefore it is imperative to learn to "speak dog". We begin the in-home teaching process with reward-based training. Each training session is always short and fun for your dog. Once the dog understands the expectations then we reinforce the good behaviors. When a calm home life has been achieved, we move onto outside training. How you live with your dog inside your home directly affects outside behaviors.

Once we establish the inside and outside training, we follow up with lots of practice. The training sessions are fun for your dog. As the improved behaviors begin to replace the old behaviors so too will your relationship be positively transformed.
